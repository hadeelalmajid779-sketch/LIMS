About the System

The Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) is a digital platform developed to improve laboratory workflow management. It allows laboratories to manage patients, medical tests, and results efficiently while reducing manual errors and improving data organization.

System Features

Patient Management

Register patients and maintain their medical records securely within the system.

Test Management

Organize laboratory tests and manage test details, departments, and sample types.

Result Tracking

Record, monitor, and approve laboratory results in an efficient digital workflow.
